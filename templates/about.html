About this site

This site is intended to provide a safe space for sharing information on dog friendly nature trails in Ireland.

Guest visitors to the site can view the post summary list without register and log-in

To register, click the Register button in navigation bar and enter required information.

Registerd and logged-in users can:
  • View the post details and leave a comment or Like the posts.
  • Create your own post to share the information of your experience on a nature trail with your four-legged friend(s).
  • View the other members profiles
